Sports management — Est. 2026

Founded in 2026 by FIFA-licensed agent Francisco de Miranda, ONZE Sports Management is a boutique football representation agency. Dutch-built and Caribbean-rooted, it was created on a single principle that has not changed since: stay close to every client — every player, every family, every association.

We represent footballers and the federations of small nations. By design the roster stays small and hand-picked: we take on few, and once we commit, we commit completely.

Our service is bespoke to each client. We advise across contract and transfer, legal, financial and tax, medical and performance, and image and brand — alongside the everyday matters of a life in the game. Quietly, thoroughly, and for the long term.
