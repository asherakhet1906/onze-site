02

FIFA Forward

Capture the full benefit

FIFA development money left on the table.

FIFA forward funding is not granted automatic. It has to be applied for, justified, accounted for with audited reports. Small federations are known to underclaim, not due to ineligibility but because they lack back office capacity to meet conditions and requirements.

What Collective XI buildsA shared compliance back office that writes applications, conducts the reporting, and files any acquittals across the entire bloc. Together, we can turn eligibility into a full and repeatable capture.

03 · The Corridor works

A true pipeline. But unmanaged. Under threat.

Diaspora players on the Seychelles squad already have homes at European clubs. But what feeds the national team today is by luck, not by design.

The wrinkle to be solved.

For example, Seychelles has strict dual nationality rules. This turns putting a national team football jersey on a diaspora prospect into a multi-jurisdictional legal structuring problem. Immigration and sports issues that our Dutch lawyers specialize in.
